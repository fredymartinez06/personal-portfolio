About Me

I am Fredy Martinez Ojeda, currently a senior at Information Technology High School located in Long Island City, NY. So far in these past couple of years I’ve been doing multiple internships that the school provides me with. After graduating this year I will be attending college. I’ve committed to SUNY Polytechnic Institution and will be majoring in Computer Science with an accelerated program aiming for a BS/MS in 5 years and on the side of that I will be minoring in psychology. I’ve chosen Computer Science as my major because I’ve been inspired by how computers have been involved and I would like to be a part of that. Also I’ve also chosen psychology as a minor because currently I like to help others and I just see myself having that capability of helping others when needed.
